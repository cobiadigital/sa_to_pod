The Constructionist Principle from Jaqualine Kelm - Appreciative Living

2022-12-11

In Jaqualine Kelm's book, Appreciative Living she further lays out the constructionist principle:

 

Life is not experienced in a vacuum. We simultaneously integrate our past and present beliefs as we construct a new reality with others. As we hear over and over from different sources that being responsible is a good way to live, we begin to view that as "truth." We come to believe that it is better to follow the rules, do what we promise, and adhere to the standards and norms of our society. We carry that truth into our next experience and act accordingly. It is only "truth"¬ù for us, from our relative perspective and framework of understanding. It will be viewed differently by others.
